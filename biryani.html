Recipe for Delicious Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 250g chicken cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 2-3 cloves, 2-3 cardamom pods, 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil/ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook rice: Rinse and cook basmati rice in 2 cups of water with a pinch of salt. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare chicken: In a pan, heat oil/ghee. Add whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon) and saut√© until fragrant. Add sliced onions and cook until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomatoes, and cook until soft.
  3. Add chicken, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is tender (about 10-15 minutes).
  4. Layer the biryani: In a large pot, layer half the cooked rice, then the cooked chicken mixture, and then the rest of the rice. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes (you can place a heavy pan under the pot to avoid direct heat).
