Recipe for Delicious Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 package (about 1 lb) pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface into a circle or rectangle.
  3. Spread sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  6. Slice and serve!

ENJOY YOUR PIZZA!
