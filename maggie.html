Recipe for Delicious Maggie

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 cup Maggi masala powder
  • Maggi tastemaker (comes with the packet)

Instructions

  1. Boil 1 1/2 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Break the Maggi noodle cake into the boiling water.
  3. Add the Maggi masala powder and stir well.
  4. Take off the heat.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the noodles are soft and the water is mostly absorbed.
  6. Stir again, and it's ready to serve!
